Privacy Policy

Effective: 15 September 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Tic Tac Toe (“the App”) handles information. The App is published by Faruk G. (“we”, “us”). If you have questions, contact farukg.dev@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

  • Ads & Analytics Data: The App uses Google services (e.g., AdMob) that may collect device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, and usage data to provide and measure ads and to combat fraud.
  • Crash/Performance Data: If enabled (e.g., Firebase), crash reports and diagnostics may be collected to improve stability.
  • No Account Data: The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect names, email addresses, or precise location.

Advertising & Consent (EEA/UK)

We use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent where required by law (e.g., GDPR in the EEA/UK). On first launch, users in applicable regions will see a consent dialog to choose how ads are personalized. You can change your choice anytime from the App’s Settings → Privacy options.

Service Providers

  • Google AdMob — mobile advertising. See Google’s partners and data usage: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.
  • Google/Firebase (optional) — analytics/crash reporting: firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Third-party services retain data per their policies.

Your Choices

  • Consent: In the EEA/UK, manage consent via Settings → Privacy options in the App.
  • Limit Ad Personalization: You can reset or limit your device’s advertising ID in Android settings.
  • Opt-Outs: See Google’s ad settings and opt-out tools at adssettings.google.com.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 16 in the EEA/UK (or the relevant age in your country). We configure UMP/AdMob to respect under-age settings.

Security

We follow reasonable practices to protect information handled by the App and its service providers.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes take effect when posted on this page. We will update the “Effective” date above.

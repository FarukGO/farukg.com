Privacy Policy

Effective: 23 October 2025

Tic Tac Toe (the “App”) is published by Faruk G (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what data is processed when you use the App, why, who processes it, and your choices.

Quick summary

  • No accounts; game data is stored locally on your device.
  • We show ads (AdMob) and use Firebase services for analytics, diagnostics, and performance.
  • In regions that require it, we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent and honor your choices. You can revisit them in Settings → Privacy & Consent.

Data we process

On-device only

Game progress, settings, and preferences are saved locally (Android DataStore). We don’t upload this to our servers.

Advertising (AdMob)

Advertising ID, IP address, device/OS info, approximate location (from IP), and ad interactions are used to display and measure ads and prevent fraud. Your choices (via UMP or system settings) control whether ads are personalized.

Analytics (Firebase)

App Instance ID and event data (e.g., screen views) help us understand usage and improve gameplay. Behavior follows your consent where required.

Diagnostics (Crashlytics & Performance)

Crash reports and performance metrics help us find and fix problems and optimize performance. Where required, we enable these only when you grant analytics consent.

Why we process data

  • Provide and improve core features (game state, UI/UX).
  • Display ads and measure ad performance (interstitial and rewarded).
  • Detect, prevent, and address crashes, abuse, or technical issues.

Your choices

  • Consent & preferences: Open Settings → Privacy & Consent in the App to review or change choices (where available).
  • Advertising ID: Reset or limit ad tracking in Android system settings.

Legal bases & regional notes

In the EEA/UK we rely on consent for ads and analytics. If you don’t consent, we show non-personalized ads and disable personalized ad signals. In US states with privacy laws governing targeted advertising, use the in-app privacy options to opt out of personalized ads.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers that enable the App (primarily Google AdMob and Firebase). We don’t sell personal information. Personalized advertising may be deemed “sharing” under some laws; you can opt out via the in-app privacy options.

Retention

We don’t keep your game data on our servers. Third-party providers retain data under their policies/settings (examples: Crashlytics ≈90 days; Firebase Analytics 2–14 months; Firebase Performance ≈30–60 days).

International transfers

Google may process data on servers outside your country (e.g., the US). See Google/Firebase privacy documentation for transfer safeguards.

Children

The App is intended for users aged 13 and over and is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App, contact us and we’ll work with our providers to address it.

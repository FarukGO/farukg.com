Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 November 2025

Tic Tac Toe: Ultimate+Infinite is published by Ethereon Studio. This policy explains what data is processed when you use the game, why it’s processed, who processes it, and your choices.

Summary Data we process Purposes & legal bases Your choices Sharing Retention Children Contact

Quick summary

  • No accounts; game state and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • We use Google AdMob to show ads and Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance) to understand usage and fix issues.
  • Where required (for example in the EEA/UK/US states), we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain and honor your consent preferences. You can revisit them in Settings → Privacy & Consent.

Data we process

On-device only

Game progress, difficulty, sound/vibration, and other preferences are saved locally (Android DataStore). We don’t run our own servers for this game.

Advertising (AdMob)

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process: Advertising ID / App Set ID, IP address (for approximate location), device/OS info, and ad interactions. Google may use this data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention; some of this data is shared within the ads ecosystem. You can opt out of personalized ads via consent and/or system settings.

Details: AdMob data disclosures

Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

Anonymous/aggregated usage events (e.g., screen views, session counts) help improve gameplay and UX. We don’t use Analytics features that link to ads unless you’ve consented where required.

Details: Firebase privacy

Diagnostics (Crashlytics & Performance)

Crash reports and performance metrics (startup time, network reliability) help us find and fix issues. These SDKs act as service providers processing data on our behalf.

Details: Firebase privacy

Why we process data & legal bases

  • App functionality & improvement (gameplay, stability, performance).
  • Advertising (showing, measuring, and improving ads; fraud prevention).

Legal bases. In the EEA/UK we rely on consent for ads and analytics. If you decline, we show non-personalized ads and limit analytics signals. Outside those regions, we rely on legitimate interests to maintain the game and prevent fraud, and consent where required by law.

Your choices

  • Consent & preferences: Open Settings → Privacy & Consent in the game to review your choices.
  • Ads personalization: Reset or limit the Advertising ID in Android Settings.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal information. We share data with service providers that enable the game—primarily Google (AdMob and Firebase). AdMob may “share” certain data within the advertising ecosystem for delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. See Google’s pages for details:

  • AdMob data disclosures
  • How Google uses information from partner apps

Retention

We don’t host your game data. Third-party providers retain data under their own policies/settings (examples: Crashlytics commonly retains reports around 90 days; Firebase Analytics retention is configurable, e.g., 2–14 months). See provider documentation for current details.

International transfers

Google may process data on servers outside your country (e.g., the US). Google provides contractual safeguards as described in their terms.

Children

This game is not directed to children. If you believe a minor has used the game, contact us and we’ll work with our providers to address it.
